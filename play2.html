Halloween is a popular horror film franchise that began with the 1978 classic Halloween, directed by John Carpenter. The story revolves around Michael Myers, a masked serial killer who, as a child, murdered his sister and later escapes from a psychiatric institution to return to his hometown of Haddonfield on Halloween night. Michael Myers is relentless and seemingly unstoppable, targeting teenagers, particularly Laurie Strode (played by Jamie Lee Curtis), who becomes his primary adversary across multiple films. The franchise is known for its suspenseful atmosphere, chilling soundtrack (composed by Carpenter), and Michael's eerie presence as he stalks his victims..
